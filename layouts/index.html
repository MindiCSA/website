Meet Mindi

Mindi Mattheus is a Board Certified Adult-Geriatric Nurse Practitioner with with training in internal medicine and cosmetic dermatology with a focus on facial rejuvenation. Known for her artful work and conservative techniques that help patients achieve facial harmony while giving them a refreshed version of their natural beauty. through education on skincare, preventative treatments and corrective treatments she has been able to create meaningful changes for patients seeking anti-aging.

Mindi completed her undergraduate studies at the University of Florida in 2008 with a Bachelors of Science in Nursing and then her Masters of Science in Nursing from New York University in 2015. She trained under world-renowned plastic surgeons, Daniel Baker, MD, and Steven Levine MD.
